Cloud Computing

Cloud computing is the on-demand availability of computer system resources. especially data storage(cloud storage) and computing power, without direct active managemet by the user. The term is generally used to describe data centers available to may users over the internet. it is the delivery of computing services incliding servers, storage, database, networking, software analytics, and intelligence over the internet("the cloud") to offer faster innovation flexible resources, and economies of scale. it is a popular opinion for people and businesses for a neumber of reaons including cost savings, increased productivity, speed and efficiency, peformance and security.
